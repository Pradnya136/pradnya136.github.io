Aikyam

Aikyam is an authentic Montessori environment which is a meticulously designed structure, arranged with scientifically designed materials, carefully planned curriculum and highly trained adults that will help your child to learn cooperative social skills, develop self-control ,learn to take initiative and develop an analytical mindset by exploring the rich possibilities of childhood.

Aikyam provides a safe, developmentally appropriate, nurturing environment that promotes social, emotional, cognitive and physical growth.


Our Vision:

● To develop a community where all children feel loved, respected and encouraged to develop their fullest potential.
● For each child to develop a curiosity of learning, discover their interests and grow in their love of learning.
● Contribute to development of strong families through parent support/fellowship and skills training.


Our Mission:

● Aikyam is in a mission is to lead and support the early learning community in building the best foundation for early learning and child care experience which promotes each child’s social/emotional, physical and cognitive development.
● Our mission is to work together to build a safe, respectful and nurturing environment focused on maximizing each child’s sense of wellbeing and acquisition of skills for life and learning.


Purpose:

To be a part of serving the early childhood development community by observing each child and facilitating their needs to achieve their individual optimum potential.
